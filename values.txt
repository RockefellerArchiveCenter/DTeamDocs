D-Team Values

Promote continuous and incremental change
Focus on the user: evaluate outcomes, not output
Build networks, not silos: be flexible and adaptable
Don't let the perfect defeat the good
Promote transparent decisions and actions by leveraging existing open-source standards and tools
Practice positive, solution-centered communication
Learn constantly by embracing failure
Be responsible and accountable to D-Team values and RAC's mission
Results over planning: create actionable and realistic goals
